About PulseBridge

Around 150,000 people die annually due to a lack of immediate first aid during medical crises. Millions more, especially elderly individuals living alone, face delayed responses when every second matters.

At Pulse Bridge, we believe your wearable should be more than a tracker — it should be a lifeline. Our mission is to push the boundaries of wearable technology, creating solutions that monitor your health and actively protect you. We streamline health tracking across any device and integrate seamlessly with any wearable, making life-saving insights accessible, unified, and effortless.

Why PulseBridge?

24/7 Surveillance

Real-time health monitoring to peace of mind for families

Medication Services

Personalized reminders and automated delivery for medicine.

Faster Responses

Prevents the risk of death from delayed medical response.

Seamless Integration

Connects to devices such as Dexcom, Apple Watch, Swoosh, etc.
