About PulseBridge

Around 150,000 people die annually due to a lack of immediate first aid during medical crises. Millions more, especially elderly individuals living alone, face delayed responses when every second matters.

PulseBridge aims to bridges the gap between personal health monitoring devices and real-time medical intervention.

Key Features

Emergancy Detection

Automatic alerts triggered by abnormal vitals or fall detection — connected to nearby healthcare responders.

Health Analytics

Cloud-based storage that summarizes and analyzes biometric data trends for smarter checkups and personalized care.

Medication Services

Provides personalized reminders and automated delivery of long-term prescriptions.

Contact Doctors

Find and contact nearby specialists based on your real-time location and health needs.
