Turning Wearables Into Lifesaving Tools — Anytime, Anywhere.


Pulse Bridge connects your health to real action.

Our app transforms everyday wearables into powerful life-saving tools — monitoring vitals like heart rate, blood pressure, and location to detect emergencies early and automatically alert healthcare services. Beyond emergency response, Pulse Bridge empowers users with features like direct access to nearby specialists, smart medication reminders with doorstep delivery, and personalized health data analytics. With seamless integration across devices and a focus on privacy, security, and accessibility, we make health protection smarter, faster, and easier — wherever life takes you.






Emergency Danger Detection

This feature automatically detects emergencies like abnormal heart rate, blood pressure spikes, or falls by analyzing live data from wearables and mobile sensors. When a danger is detected, it instantly alerts nearby healthcare responders and shares the user’s real-time location for rapid intervention.

Contact & Locate Doctors

Instantly find and contact nearby specialists based on your real-time location and current health conditions. Whether you're traveling or in an unfamiliar area, our system intelligently matches you with verified medical professionals who are qualified to handle your specific needs.

Medication Reminder & Delivery

Never miss a dose again. Our app provides personalized reminders based on prescription schedules and ensures timely, automated delivery of long-term medications right to your door — so users can stay healthy without the hassle.

Health Data Analytics

Our platform securely stores biometric data and continuously analyzes trends in heart rate, blood pressure, activity, and more. This enables smarter, more personalized care through predictive insights, monthly summaries, and easy doctor access during checkups.
